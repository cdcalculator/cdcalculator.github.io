About Compound Interest

Compound interest is the eighth wonder of the world according to Albert Einstein. It's the interest calculated on the initial principal and also on the accumulated interest from previous periods. This means you earn interest on your interest, creating exponential growth over time.

The compounding frequency significantly impacts your returns. Daily compounding generates slightly more than monthly, which generates more than quarterly or annual compounding. The formula accounts for both your regular contributions and the compound growth of your investment.

Understanding compound interest is essential for wealth building. Starting early and making regular contributions can lead to substantial wealth accumulation over time, even with modest investment amounts.
