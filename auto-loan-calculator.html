About Auto Loans

Auto loans are secured loans used to purchase vehicles. The car serves as collateral, which typically results in lower interest rates compared to unsecured loans. Most auto loans range from 36 to 72 months, with longer terms resulting in lower monthly payments but higher total interest costs.

A larger down payment reduces your loan amount and can help you secure better interest rates. Your credit score significantly impacts the interest rate you'll receive - excellent credit can save thousands in interest over the life of the loan.

Consider the total cost of ownership beyond the monthly payment, including insurance, maintenance, and fuel costs. A good rule of thumb is to keep your total vehicle expenses under 20% of your monthly income.
