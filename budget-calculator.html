About Budget Planning

A budget is a financial plan that helps you manage income and expenses. Following the 50/30/20 rule is popular: allocate 50% to needs, 30% to wants, and 20% to savings and debt repayment.

Your housing costs should ideally not exceed 30% of your gross income. Tracking expenses helps identify areas where you can cut back and save more. Regular budget reviews ensure you stay on track with financial goals.

Building an emergency fund of 3-6 months of expenses should be a priority. Once established, focus on retirement savings and debt elimination for long-term financial health.
