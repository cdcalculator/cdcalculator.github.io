About Debt Payoff Strategies

Paying off debt strategically can save thousands in interest and help you achieve financial freedom faster. The two popular methods are the Debt Avalanche (paying off highest interest rate first) and Debt Snowball (paying off smallest balance first).

Making extra payments, even small amounts, can dramatically reduce your payoff time and total interest paid. Consider cutting expenses, using windfalls, or earning extra income to accelerate your debt elimination journey.
