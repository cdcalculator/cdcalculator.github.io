About Debt Payoff Strategies

The Debt Avalanche method focuses on paying off debts with the highest interest rates first while making minimum payments on others. This strategy saves the most money in interest charges and is mathematically the fastest way to become debt-free.

The Debt Snowball method targets the smallest debts first, providing quick wins and psychological motivation. While it may cost slightly more in interest, many people find this approach more motivating as they see debts eliminated quickly.

Making extra payments, even small amounts, can dramatically reduce your payoff timeline and save thousands in interest. Consistency is key - choose a strategy that matches your personality and stick with it until you're debt-free.
