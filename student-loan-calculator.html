About Student Loans

Student loans help finance education but require careful repayment planning. Most federal student loans offer a 6-month grace period after graduation before payments begin, though interest may accrue during this time.

Standard repayment plans span 10 years, but extended plans up to 25 years are available for lower monthly payments at the cost of higher total interest. Income-driven repayment plans base payments on your income and family size.

Consider making extra payments toward principal to reduce total interest costs. Even small additional payments can significantly shorten your repayment timeline and save thousands in interest.
