About Retirement Planning

Retirement planning is crucial for ensuring financial security in your later years. This calculator helps you project how much money you'll have saved by retirement based on your current savings, monthly contributions, and expected investment returns.

The calculator accounts for inflation, which erodes purchasing power over time. The "real value" shows what your retirement savings will be worth in today's dollars. A 7% return rate is historically average for diversified stock portfolios, while 2.5% inflation represents long-term averages.

Financial experts recommend saving 15-20% of your income for retirement. The earlier you start saving, the more time compound interest has to work in your favor. Consider maximizing employer 401(k) matches and contributing to IRAs for tax advantages.
